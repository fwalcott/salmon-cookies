About Us

Salmon cookies started 10 years ago in Seattle, Washington, USA. It started with a family recipe combining our love of sweets and the salmon fisheries that Washington state is known for. There we came up with Salmon Cookies. We sold them at John's Bakery as a fluke to see if anyone outside our friends and family would like this strange fishy treat. It was an immediate hit. In 10 years, we now have five stores in five cities worldwide, and we are only getting started.
